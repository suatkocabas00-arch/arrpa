Data Deletion Instructions

Arrpa users may request deletion of their account and associated data by following the process below.

Processing time: up to 7 days Applies to Arrpa accounts

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to contact@arrpa.app.
  2. Include your registered phone number.
  3. If available, include your Arrpa username or account details.
  4. Wait for confirmation after your request is verified.

What Will Be Deleted

Once verified, Arrpa will permanently delete the user account and associated data from active systems, unless retention is required for legal, fraud prevention, or security reasons.
