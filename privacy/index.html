Privacy Policy

This page explains what information Arrpa collects, how it is used, and how we protect user data across our platform and authentication systems.

Last updated: 2026 Applies to Arrpa platform services

Arrpa is a livestock and farm management platform that helps users manage animals, feeding, health records, and marketplace activities.

Information We Collect

  • Phone number for account login and verification
  • User profile information
  • Farm, livestock, feeding, and health-related records
  • Device and session information for security and login protection

How We Use Information

We use collected information to provide access to the Arrpa platform, authenticate users with OTP systems, improve platform functionality, maintain account security, and reduce abuse or fraud.

Data Sharing

Arrpa does not sell personal data. Information may be processed by infrastructure and authentication providers only when necessary to deliver platform functionality, security, and legal compliance.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.
